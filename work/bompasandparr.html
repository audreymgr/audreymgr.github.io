Bompas & Parr – Chladni Bowl

  • The Chladni Bowl, exhibited at London Merge Festival.

    (stainless steel, crystal ball, plywood, Earthquake Q10-B, hardwood timber, paint)

    product design / sound design / video design - 2015 - London, Amsterdam x Stedelijk Museum (What Design Can Do)

  • Bompas & Parr asked me to bring to life a legendary punchbowl dreamt by Johannes Kepler to illustrate movements of the planets towards each other. Through this, he was meant to demonstrate the existence of the Solar System.

    This punchbowl had to be as extraordinary as possible; we therefore decided to combine Kepler's and Chladni's experiments, by making the entire structure vibrate through the frequencies of sub-bass. Fluids follow the vibrations of music to create eerie patterns inside the punchbowl.

  • The Chladni Bowl in use at the Barbican during Into the Unknown, A Journey through Science Fiction

  • A detailed view of one of my 3D prototypes of the Chladni Bowl, following Kepler's Model of the Universe; the golden centre is representing the Sun.

Bompas & Parr – Alcoholic Architecture

  • Alcoholic Architecture is a bar where monks meet Miami, ghosts speak in loos, and snakes destroy castles. But beyond all, Alcoholic Architecture is a bar where you do not drink alcohol... you breathe it. It's the world's first breathable cloud of cocktail, previously based in Broadway Market, London.
