Miscellaneous – Sharpen Hours

  • I have been working on an absurd debate game, based on Schopenhauer's The Art of Being Right: 38 Ways to Win an Argument.

  • I piled his arguments into different 'action' cards, which are to be used in combination to a given topic to debate over.

  • As these arguments are rather difficult to pull off or properly dishonest, I decided to make the game as ridiculous and meaningless as possible.

  • Therefore, each time two debaters pick a topic to debate over, they also are obliged to pick 'personalities' cards to combine with the type of arguments they will use.

  • I explored the 100 most popular topics of debate in recent history, and branded each with a 'value' factor -- the trickiest this theme is to debate, the more rewarding it will be to win it over.

  • Sharpen Hours is currently under user testing and further development;
