Current workplace

  • DT is an automotive social media website featuring The Grand Tour presenters.

    The website launched in November 2016 with several selected tribe leaders to get things started. These early adopters started their tribes a week ahead of the full release to allow new fan made tribes to reach maturity before the masses are allowed entry. Each tribe has a specific theme and members join whichever tribe fits their personal tastes. The trio were joined by several automotive celebrities such as Mark Webber, Damon Hill, and many others.

    Please visit route1.co to visualise my work on the website.

Webtool

  • Route1's second digital product is a webtool for legal employers. The aim of this hub is to make the experience of hiring lawyers through our app completely friction-free and simple. I took care of re-designing the entirety of both the UX and the UI of the hub, in order to make it as minimal and modern as possible.

    The Hub 2.0 is currently under production.
