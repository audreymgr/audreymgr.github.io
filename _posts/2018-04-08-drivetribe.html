DriveTribe

  • DriveTribe is an automotive social media website created by Jeremy Clarkson, James May and Richard Hammond.

    The website and mobile app launched in November 2016, with several selected tribe leaders to get things started. These early adopters started their tribes a week ahead of the full release to allow new fan-made tribes to reach maturity before the masses are allowed entry. Each tribe has a specific theme and members join whichever tribe fits their personal tastes. The trio were joined by several automotive celebrities such as Mark Webber, Damon Hill, and many others.

    Please visit their website to visualise my work on the web product.

  • DriveTribe currently holds over 1 million registered users, and a whopping 3 billion impressions per month. It has an excellent rating on both Google Play and the App Store.

    Available for downloads on The App Store or Google Play.

    Joyride

  • The average session length on Joyride is, and has been since launch in May, over 7 minutes long. The pool of users is still quite small, around 8,000 weekly users. But the stickiness of Joyride is undeniable; especially compared to DriveTribe. I am very proud that I played a big part in conceiving and shipping this app.

    Available for downloads on The App Store or Google Play.
