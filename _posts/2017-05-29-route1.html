Webtool

  • Route1's second digital product is a webtool for legal employers. The aim of this hub is to make the experience of hiring lawyers through our app completely friction-free and simple. I took care of re-designing the entirety of both the UX and the UI of the hub, in order to make it as minimal and modern as possible.

    The Hub 2.0 is currently under production.
